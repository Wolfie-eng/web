Des mots. Des vies. Des changements.

It's disturbing how men of every age treat women as their toy. And it's more disturbing to see how people treat you when you use your voice to tell your story. We WOMEN have to be perfect each second and all the time. We can't cry, we can't feel sad, we have to work, to help... We can't be Human. But I am human, and I want to change things. I hope this message will help each person who will read it. You don't have to be perfect You can feel sad You don't have to always run And your voice has power You matter.
